# 📺 Watch Alongside — Your Indigo League Viewing Guide {.type-normal}

This workbook is built to be read **with the first-generation Pokémon anime — the Indigo League — playing alongside it.** Each chapter tracks specific episodes: the scene that raises the chapter's question is the same scene that motivates its math. Ash meets the Spearow swarm and you learn to reason about "at least one"; he reaches Cerulean and you learn to reason from clues; the League fills with thousands of trainers and you learn why the bell curve appears. **Story and statistics reinforce each other** — watching makes the math stick, and the math makes the watching richer.

**How to use it.** Watch the listed episode(s) for a chapter **before** you read it (or right after) — twenty-odd minutes of show, then the chapter that turns that adventure into a skill. Every chapter also opens with a **📺 Now Playing** box repeating its episode, so you can simply move through the book and watch as you go. The mapping follows the anime's spirit faithfully; where the book extends the story to serve a problem, it says so ("*in-world extension*"). **You never *need* the show** — the book is complete on its own — but if you want the journey to feel like Kanto, press play.
