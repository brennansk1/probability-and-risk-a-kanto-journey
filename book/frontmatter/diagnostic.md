# The Skip Diagnostic — Test Out of Any Chapter

*(Front matter — to be finalized by the writing workflow.)*

Think you already know a chapter? **Don't read it — fight it.** Go straight to that chapter's **Gym Challenge** problem set. Work it timed at six minutes per question, then check the full solutions in the Answer Key.

- Score the chapter's stated mastery bar (typically **80%+ with correct method**) and you may skip ahead.
- Miss it, and the chapter is waiting for you — work it in full.

The exam doesn't care what you *recognize* — only what you can *do* under time. The skip test measures exactly that. Each chapter closes with a **Badge Earned** checklist of can-do statements; if any fails, the "Gym rematch" pointer tells you which section to revisit.
